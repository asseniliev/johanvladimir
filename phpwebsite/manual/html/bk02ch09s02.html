Listing Albums

When you first enter the Photo Album you will see a listing of all the Photo Albums which are currently in the database. From this list you can go to view each individual album. In this album view you can see thumnail images of all the photos in the album. At the top of the page with this list are two links. The first link is for listing albums, it will to always bring you back to this list. The other link says New Album and will take you to the form you need to add a new photo album to the database.
