Getting Started

Blocks are one of the most basic components of a web site. Blocks created by blockmaker are to be used for placing small amounts of content on your site. If the content is going to be longer than just a few sentences then consider making a pagemaster page. If you have decided a block is what you need then navigate to the administrative side of the Block Maker module. The Block Maker module can be found under the Site Content Tab of your Control Panel. The link for it says Block Maker and has an icon of a cursor hovering over a series of blocks.

  • Click on the textual link or the icon to continue.
