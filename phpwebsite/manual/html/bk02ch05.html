Getting Started

Menus within phpWebSite are used for site navigation. To begin using the Menu Manager module you must go to the adminisrative side. The menu manager can be found under the Site Content tab of your controlpanel. The link says Menu Manager and may have an icon that looks like an open menu.

  • Click on the textual link or the icon to continue.
